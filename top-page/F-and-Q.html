Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about thriving in Japan

What is the Thrive in Japan program?

The Thrive in Japan program is made up of TWO curriculums designed to enhance students' high-context communication skills, crucial for navigating Japan's unique cultural landscape. It prepares participants for both personal and professional success in Japan. While addressing all requirements for JLPT certification it doesn't get caught up in them.

What if I already have JLPT but I still want to learn to Thrive?

"Japan in Context" can be studied as part of the monthly subscription, and it will include pre-recorded videos, reflections, and some of the group work will be integrated into the Speaking Sessions, however if you are really struggling and desperate to change your life the only way to get our full guarantee is by taking the "Japan in Context" program with our Professional ICF Certified Coaching.
If you are serious about finding your purpose and living true to yourself while living in Japan (at this point you might feel stuck here), then this is the magic bullet you've been looking for.

Who can benefit from the Thrive in Japan program?

The program is ideal for anyone who is pursuing higher education or a career in Japan, or are planning on spending time in Japan in the near future and need to get the most out of their time. Not all experiences are created equal, and the only way to max out your experience in Japan is to understand the context. Students that have experienced Japan before and felt the language and cultural barriers for themselves first hand will benefit all the more from the program but even if you haven't been to Japan yet, this program will shave years off of your learning curve.

What if I speak advanced Japanese? What if I'm a beginner?

High-Context Communication in Japanese is mostly unspoken. Whether you decide to take our "JLPT in Context" Course to learn Japan is up to you. The high-context communication skills that we teach in our "Japan in Context" program applies to everyone, and it doesn't matter the level of your Japanese skills to benefit from these lessons.

Do you actually teach Japanese Language?

Yes, we do, "JLPT in Context" is a ground-breaking curriculum that (in our opinion) leaves other programs in the dust. BUT before we start teaching you the language (no matter how fluent you already are) we encourage that ALL of our students go through the "Japan in Context" course.
Japanese is at least 60% unspoken communication skills, tools and strategies. The remaining 40% is made up of words and grammar structures.
Learning Japanese with us is a decision to redefine your terms. What is the Japanese Language? Is it only vocabulary words and grammar? As far as we are concerned, "no" - Our students learn Japanese as a holistic communication SYSTEM. This system cannot be ignored, and Japanese cannot be treated and taught in the same way that other languages are traditionally taught in academic settings. To Thrive in Japan you will need to take a more balanced approach to learning than a simple JLPT course can provide.
